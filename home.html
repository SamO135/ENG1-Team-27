Unity takes place in an alternate universe where the University of York (UoY) is built around a large lake and the only way to travel between colleges is by boat.

The aim of the game is to defeat the evil Contantine College, while also avoiding being sunk by rival colleges/pirates.

Points may be accumulated passively by sailing around the map, or through in-game action (e.g. sinking enemy vessels, or defeating/capturing enemy colleges); the player can also gain plunder through attacking enemy ships and colleges.
